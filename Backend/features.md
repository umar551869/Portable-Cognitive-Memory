# 🌟 Portable Cognitive Graph: The Future of Personal Intelligence

What started as a visionary concept in a Jupyter Notebook has now been forged into a high-performance, industrial-grade reality. The **Portable Cognitive Graph (PCG)** is not just a tool; it is a phenomenal advancement in how humans and AI interface with memory.

## 🚀 Phenomenal Core Features

### 🧠 1. Autonomous Cognitive Ingestion (The Watcher)
The PCG sits silently in the shadows of your operating system. Using advanced filesystem hooks, it monitors your every move. The moment you save a file—a thought, a code snippet, or a decision—the "Watcher" captures it instantly. No manual logging. No friction. Pure autonomous memory capture.

### 🧬 2. LLM-Driven Dynamic Synthesis
We don't just store text; we extract **meaning**. Every piece of data is funneled through the state-of-the-art **Gemini 1.5 Flash** "Brain." It identifies entities, maps their relationships, and weaves them into a dense tapestry of knowledge. It understands that "Jarvis" is a project, a tool, and a partner—all at once.

### 🌐 3. 3D Portable Graphical Memory
Witness your mind in 3D. The PCG transforms abstract database rows into a stunning, interactive **3D Force-Directed Graph**. With a single command, you can fly through your past, visualize the clusters of your expertise, and see exactly how your ideas are interconnected. It is a breathtaking window into your own cognitive evolution.

### ⛓️ 4. Semantic Deduplication & Merging
The PCG is smarter than a standard database. Using **pgvector** and high-dimensional embeddings, it recognizes when two different notes are talking about the same thing. It automatically merges aliases, weights existing concepts, and creates a "Canonical Truth" for your life's work.

### 🛫 5. Universal Portability & Cloud Persistence
Your memory is no longer bound to a single machine. By utilizing a **cloud-backed Supabase architecture**, your cognitive graph is globally accessible. Move from PC to PC, or plug in an entirely new AI agent—your history is instantly available via a single secure connection string.

### 🔍 6. Semantic Recall (The "Oracle" Interface)
The PCG isn't just for storage; it's for **Recall**. Through our semantic search interface, you can ask the system natural language questions. It performs vector-similarity lookups to find the exact context you need, effectively giving any AI agent you use a "Total Recall" capability of your entire history.
